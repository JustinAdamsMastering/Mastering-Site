About me

I've always loved music. When I was around ten years old, I started playing hand percussion in a “family band” with my dad and five (out of six) siblings. Over the years, I've picked up percussion, guitar, piano, and bass(ish). Any time there is a new instrument in sight, I like to try it out; probably the most interesting one I've gotten to try so far is the hurdy-gurdy.

As I got a little older, I started getting into composing music (mostly orchestral film style) on an old 1990s digital Alesis keyboard recorded into Cubase. After that, I moved into an EDM phase in FL Studio. As time went on, I became more and more dissatisfied with how my music sounded... That's when I discovered mixing. I became obsessed with making music sound as good as I could, and pivoted away from composition to audio engineering. For 10+ years, I've studied/practiced/experimented with audio engineering (The last 5 or so of which I switched focus to mastering).

After years of trial and error with blood, sweat, and tears, I finally feel like I've become a very capable mastering engineer and am excited to collaborate and help you get your song sounding exactly how you want!
